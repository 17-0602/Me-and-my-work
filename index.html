About me

Hi there! My name is Eréndira, but you can call me Eren (It's shorter and less hard to pronounce). I'm a 25 year-old translator from Mexico City. Wait...A translator?!

Yes! I studied translation at Instituto Superior de Intérpretes y Traductores in Mexico City. In 2020 I got my bachelor's degree in Translation and, in 2021, I studied terminology.

I decided to study translation because I love languages, to read, and to correct grammar mistakes. When I was in high school, I was so passionate about languages that I started to learn French and Japanese at the same time. Nowadays, I speak English, French, Japanese, Italian and, my mother tongue, Spanish.

I want to continue studying languages, but from another kind like HTML, Phyton, Java... 😃
